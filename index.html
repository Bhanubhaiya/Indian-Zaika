üßà Butter Chicken

Ingredients:
  • For Marinade:
    • 500g boneless chicken (cut into chunks)
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 tsp red chili powder
    • 1/2 tsp turmeric
    • Salt to taste
    • Juice of half lemon
  • For Gravy:
    • 2 tbsp butter
    • 1 tbsp oil
    • 1 onion (finely chopped)
    • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 cups tomato puree
    • 1 tsp garam masala
    • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
    • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
    • 1 tsp sugar
    • 1/2 cup fresh cream
    • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (crushed)
    • Salt to taste
Instructions:
  1. Marinate chicken with all marinade ingredients. Keep for at least 1 hour (or overnight).
  2. Grill or pan-fry marinated chicken until slightly charred.
  3. In a pan, heat butter and oil. Saut√© onions till golden, add ginger-garlic paste, cook 2 mins.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Add cumin, coriander powder, sugar, and salt. Cook 3-4 mins.
  6. Add grilled chicken, garam masala, and kasuri methi.
  7. Add cream, simmer for 5-10 mins. Serve with naan or rice.

üçõNon-veg Biryani

Ingredients:
  • 500g meat (chicken/lamb/goat)
  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked 30 mins)
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices: 2 bay leaves, 4 cloves, 2 cardamoms, 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp red chili powder, salt
  • Saffron strands soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped mint and coriander
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil
Instructions:
  1. Marinate meat with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, salt, chili powder, and turmeric.
  2. Fry onions until brown and crispy. Reserve half for layering.
  3. Cook the marinated meat in a heavy pan with some fried onions until tender.
  4. In another pot, boil rice with whole spices. Cook until 70% done. Drain.
  5. In a heavy pot, layer cooked meat ‚Üí rice ‚Üí fried onions ‚Üí herbs ‚Üí saffron milk.
  6. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20‚Äì25 mins (dum method).
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken legs (make deep cuts)
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients into a marinade. Coat chicken well and refrigerate overnight.
  2. Preheat oven to 200¬∞C (or use a grill/pan).
  3. Bake for 30‚Äì35 mins or until charred and cooked, basting with oil halfway.
  4. Serve with lemon wedges and onion rings.

ü•î Masala Dosa

Ingredients:

Dosa Batter:
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1/2 cup urad dal
  • Salt
  • Water
Potato Filling:
  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Batter: Soak rice and dal separately for 6 hours. Grind and mix together with salt. Ferment overnight.
  2. Potato Filling: Heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chili, and onions. Saut√©. Add turmeric and mashed potatoes. Mix and cook for 2‚Äì3 mins.
  3. To Make Dosa: Heat non-stick pan, spread batter thinly, drizzle oil. When crisp, place filling and fold.
  4. Serve with sambar and coconut chutney.

üå∞ Pani Puri

Ingredients:

  • Puri: Store-bought or homemade (semolina-based)

Filling:

  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • Salt, chili powder, cumin

Pani (Spiced Water):

  • Mint + coriander leaves
  • Tamarind pulp
  • Green chilies
  • Black salt, cumin powder, chaat masala
  • Cold water

Instructions:

  1. Grind mint, coriander, tamarind, and spices. Mix with cold water.
  2. Mix potato and chickpeas with spices.
  3. Crack puri top, add filling, dip in pani, and eat immediately.

ü•ü Samosas

Ingredients:

Dough:

  • 2 cups maida (flour)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt, water

Filling:

  • 4 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • Ginger, green chili
  • Garam masala, amchur, salt

Instructions:

  1. Make stiff dough using flour, salt, oil, and water. Rest 30 mins.
  2. Saut√© cumin, ginger, chili, add peas and potatoes with spices.
  3. Roll dough into ovals, cut in half, form cones, fill, and seal.
  4. Deep fry on medium flame until golden brown.

ü´ò Dal Makhani

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup whole urad dal
  • 1/4 cup rajma (kidney beans)
  • 2 tbsp butter + 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup cream

Instructions:

  1. Soak dal & rajma overnight. Pressure cook till soft.
  2. Saut√© onion, garlic, and tomatoes in butter.
  3. Add chili powder, salt, dal, and simmer 30‚Äì45 mins.
  4. Add cream and simmer 10 mins more. Serve with naan or jeera rice.

ü•¨ Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 cups spinach (blanched & pureed)
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato, 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt, cream

Instructions:

  1. Blanch and puree spinach.
  2. In oil, fry cumin, onion, paste, and tomato. Cook well.
  3. Add spinach puree, salt, garam masala. Cook 5 mins.
  4. Add paneer and simmer. Add a splash of cream.

ü•ï Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 onion, 1 tomato
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, etc.)
  • Ginger-garlic paste, biryani masala
  • Mint and coriander, saffron milk

Instructions:

  1. Saut√© onions and whole spices. Add paste, tomato, and yogurt.
  2. Add vegetables and cook till soft.
  3. Boil rice to 70%. Drain.
  4. Layer vegetable curry and rice, top with herbs and saffron milk.
  5. Dum cook for 20 mins. Serve with raita.
